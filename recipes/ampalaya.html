Ginisang Ampalaya

Description

When met with the word Ampalaya (especially) in the Philippines, one likely associates it with bitterness — after all, it is named bitter gourd. However, do not mistake it for that alone; it can be turned into a flavorful dish!

Ingredients

  • 2 pcs ampalaya, sliced
  • 1 pc tomato, sliced
  • 1 pc onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 pcs egg, beaten
  • salt, pepper, and Ginisa Flavor Seasoning Mix to taste

Steps

Preparation

  • Soak bitter gourd in water mixed with salt for about 15 minutes.

Cooking Proper

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Sauté in this order: onion, garlic, tomato, and then bitter gourd.
  3. Add salt, ginisa mix, and pepper to taste.
  4. Cover and cook for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Add the eggs and mix them with the other ingredients until cooked.

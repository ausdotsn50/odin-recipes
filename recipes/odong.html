Odong

Description

With its quick preparation time and minimal ingredient requirements, Odong is a great go-to dish that you can opt to prepare anytime at home.

Ingredients

  • 4 20g odong noodles
  • 2 cans of spicy sardines in tomato sauce
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 cans of water (use sardines can)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a cooking pot.
  2. Once the oil is hot, saut√© the garlic and onion until the onion softens.
  3. Add the sardines with their sauce and pour in water. Bring to a boil before adding the noodles.
  4. Cook the noodles for 5 to 8 minutes, or until they are tender.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste, then stir.

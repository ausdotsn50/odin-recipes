Pork Sinigang

Description

The Filipino dish 'Sinigang' has several variations depending on the type of meat used. There's Fish Sinigang, Chicken Sinigang, and Pork Sinigang â€” a personal favorite, to name a few. Taking a sip of its magical soup will send you to the asim-kilig portals!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, cubed
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 1 medium ginger, quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 medium radish, sliced
  • 1 1.55oz Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix Gabi
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1 bunch kangkong or bokchoy or spinach

Steps

  1. In a pot over medium heat, bring the water to a boil.
  2. Add the onion, ginger, and tomatoes.
  3. Once the water is boiling, add the pork and cook until tender.
  4. Stir in the Sinigang mix.
  5. Add the radish and continue to cook.
  6. Reduce the heat and simmer for 5 minutes before adding the kangkong, bok choy, or spinach.
